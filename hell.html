HELL IS FOR SATAN AND HIS AGENTS



Hell is an Everlasting fire, prepared for the devil and his angels .
A furnance of fire, a place where there is no water, where worms dieth not or fire quenched.







FIRST DAY IN HELL



EVERYONE will stand at the entrance of the everlasting torment:

When your name is not found written in the book of life, an angel of distruction will lead you to the entrance gate of hell, where you will see many people standing waiting to enter into eternal death of sorrow.

People are crying beging for forgiveness and second chance.

Many people will be crying beging for second chance,"but is too late" young and old, male and female all in sorrow and tears shouting "Had i know" .

THE floor beneath is getting hotter, yet your skin is intact but full of pain.

Fire will be everywhere burning but the skin will never get burnt, the same fire burning a lier is the same fire burning a murderer.
