WAYS TO DRAW CLOSE TO GOD

*WORSHIP: We draw close to God through worship, God loves when we glorify his holy name.
Worship will make his eyes to be upon us day and night.
Exodus 34:14-for thou worship no other god: for the LORD, Whose name is jealous, is a jealouse GOD.
*OBEYING GOD's COMMAND: when we obey God's commandment, it create a bond of love between us and God, he will never allow us to fall into danger
*PRAYER: if we always pray to God it automatically connect us with God like a bluetooth.

YES! God knows all things, Some situation comes to us as a test to our faith,

But when he shows us mercy he will never allow the situation to over power us.
Some situations comes to draw us back to God, especially when we think we are okey in every aspect of live.
Nahum 1:7-The LORD is good, a strong hold in the day of trouble; and he knoweth them that trust in him.
