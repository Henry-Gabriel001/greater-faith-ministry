HEALING


And said, if thou wilt diligently hearken to the voice of the LORD thy GOD, and wilt do that which is right in his sight, and wilt give ear to his commandment, and keep all his statutes, i will put none of these diseases upon thee, which i have brought upon the egyptians, for i am the LORD that healeth thee.

VERSE FOR HEALING

psalms 147:3-
He healeth the broken in heart, and bindeth up thier wounds.
James 5:14-
is any sick among you? let him call for the elders of the church, and let them pray over him, anointing him with oil in the name of the LORD Jesus.
