FAITH AND FEAR




Faith is a powerful and complex concept that can bring comfort, strength, and guildance to many people . it can be a source of hope, resilience, and inspiration.
Faith means believing in what God has reveal.
Fear A strong and universal emotion, fear is a natural response to perceived threats or dangers, and it can be overwhelming and debilitating.
Fear means having doubt of overcoming or facing every situation.




Here are some key differences between fear and faith.

  • Fear is focused on the negative, while faith is focused on tht positive

    Fear is often accompanied by anxiety, worry or dread, while faith is often accompanied by peace, hope and joy.

    Fear can lead to avoidance or control, while faith can lead to action , surrender or trust.
