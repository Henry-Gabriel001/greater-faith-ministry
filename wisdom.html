GOD'S WISDOM


God's wisdom is the comprehensive knowledge and understanding that God has of all things.
According to the Bible , some ways to gain God's wisdom include: fearing God : proverb 9:10 that the fear of the lord is the beginning of wisdom.
God's wisdom is seeing the world from God's perspective and then applying that perspective to our lives.
proverbs 3:19 says,"The lord by wisdom founded the earth, established the heavens by understanding. " when we seek wisdom, we seek to know God's mind and act according to his unchanging truth.
God's wisdom is the ability to see beyound the material world and to understand the interconnectedness of all things.


EARTHLY WISDOM


Earthly wisdom is a mindset that is based on selfish ambition and jealousy, and is in contrast to Godly wisdom.
Characteriistics
Earthly wisdom is based on the sences and emotions, and is often characterized by bitter envy and selfish ambition.
it is also the result of a lust for power or inordinate desire for things that do not belong to us .
Examples:
Some examples of earthly wisdom includes following your heart, seeing is believing, and loving your family and friends.
