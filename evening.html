Unveiling the Great and mighty

In the midst of life's challenges and uncertainties, we often find ourselves seeking answer and solutions. we ponder over the future, seeking clarity and understanding. And in these moments of seeking, we are reminded of the promise in Jeremiah 33:3
Call unto me, and i will answer thee, and shew thee great and mighty things, which thou knowest not.

PRAYER

Father we thank you for your love and protection throughout the day, we ask that you shall protect us with your mighty hand of power so that our enemies will never have dominion over us, thank you father for your provisions let your name be glorify in jesus mighty name we pray. Amen.
